M D Pallavi & Arun

She is a Kannada Sugama Sangeetha Singer, playback Singer, actor and TV Anchor.  Arun, a musician and music director.

Pallavi won the Karnataka State film awards for Best playback singer in 2006-07 for her song Nodayya Kwate Lingave sung film Duniya.  

Has shared the stage with acclaimed musicians like Late.MysoreAnanthaswamy, S.P.Balasubramanyam, Jesudas, C.Ashwath, Amith Heri.

Won Best Singer of the Year Athri award, Suryodaya Trust Best Playback Singer.
