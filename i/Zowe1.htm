The only Neo-Classical male voice group in the country. Started in October 2009 with a vision to promote music of different genres. Zowe Madrigal is led by Nise Meruno. The Group consists of people from varied background but strong and dedicated people. After its inception, the group has been invited to perform for a number of shows,concerts and weddings., Viz, A Night At The Broadway (First Concert), Hornbill Festival 2009.2010..etc..

Zowe Madrigal biggest achievement till date would be the opportunity to perform for The President of India in Dec 2009 and Dec 2010 at The Rashtrapati Bhavan. They were well received by the audience as well as the media. Some of the members also had the privilege to perform for the third time during The President's maiden visit to Nagaland 2011.

Zowe Madrigal led by Nise Meruno recently gave a successful concert in Guwahati under the aegis of Yamaha Music company. They also performed in Delhi at the India Habitat Centre and The Neemrana Fort Palace in June 2011. Zowe Madrigal had the privilege to perform with French Dancer/Choreographer Gilles Chuyen and India's best known Soprano Situ Singh Buehler.

The Concert tour was supported by the Neemrana Music Foundation and Kawai Grand Piano company. Zowe Madrigal repertoire includes Western Classical, Sacred, Jazz, Contemporary, Popular Classics, Bollywood,. etc.However the Group's forte lies in Naga Folk Fusion originals. Mostly arranged and composed by The Director Nise Meruno.
