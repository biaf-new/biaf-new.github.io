B. Bhanumati: Bharatanatyam Dancer/Choreographer/ Mentor

Born in a musically inclined family. Mother: Isamani L.R.Lakshmi, noted musician composer; Father: Dr C.S.Balakrishnan : a zealous promoter of the cause of Indian Classical Music.

B. Bhanumati started learning dance at the age of 10. Privilege of the tutelage of greats like Padmashri K.N.Dandayuthapani Pillai at Chennai,Padmabhushan Dr.K.Venkatalakshamma, the doyen of Mysore Palace tradition and Padmabhushan Smt. Kalanidhi Narayanan

Landmark achievements :

Titles:

Kalaa Shri: Karnataka Sangeeta Nritya Academy: 2000

Rajyotsava Award: Govt of Karnataka: 2001;

Chanakya Kautilya Award: Chanakya Foundation: 2006;

Aryabhata Award: Aryabhata Cultural Organisation: 2007;

Natyakala Tapasvini: Bharatiya Vidya Bhavan, Sridevi Nrityakendra, Mangalore: 2010;

 

Academic:

Member, Text Book Committee on Dance, Karnataka State Secondary Edn.Board;

External Examiner for Post gradute students in Bangalore University

Archival Documentation on Dance Gurus Padmashri Dandayuthapani Pillai and Padmabhushan Venkatalakshamma by Shruti foundation, Chennai and Sangeeta Nritya Academy, Bangalore, respectively.

Solo Performances:

Over 2000 programs in India & abroad;

Participation in all major dance festivals

 

Guru B. Bhanumati's Most Popular Group Dance Feature Bharatanjali has seen over 700 shows in all major national dance festivals of India and several tours to the UK and USA for performances as well as cultural exchange programs.
