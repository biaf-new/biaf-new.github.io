Evam Standup Comedy

A Standup style show promising a hilarious evening, with tall tales ranging from


first time USA visit to anguish of VISA rejects,
from Nepali gurkhas to Coimbatore culture shocks,
first kisses,
finding apartments,
film shoots,
flimsy food,


south indian arranged marriage syndrome and pets that were set to take over the world - this show  promises to provide enough food for thought and more. Come, meet some very interesting people and watch them narrate their urban stories, thoughts, theories and peeves.

 A completely original Evam show - this indian, urban perspective is meant to tickle your funny bone and create post-show debate.

Evam - Evam entertainment is an award winning theatre entrepreneurship which has  the mission of  "revolutionising india's theatre".

 Founded in 2003 by two young graduates from MICA, Ahmedabad. Evam today is a 9 year young thriving organization with more than 25 plays and 450 performances. Evam has travelled across India and also the globe.
