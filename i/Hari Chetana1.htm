Noopur is the essence of dance in all its variety and richness. Being one of the foremost Performing Art Center formed by Hari and Chethana, established in the year 1996.

 

Noopur having a goal to spread dance among all the strata of society, has been performing Indian classical Kathak Dance form at various prestigious dance festivals in India and Abroad.

 

Noopur Performing Art Centre Bangalore is one of the pioneer dance companies of International acclaim.

 

Hari and Chethana have distinguished themselves as inspiring dancers and have  given  performances all over India, United states of America, Canada, the United Kingdom, Germany, Indonesia, France, Switzerland, Singapore, Thailand, Malaysia, Saudi- Arabia, Srilanka and the Middle – East and have won critical acclaim around the globe

 

They are empanelled artistes of Indian Council for Cultural Relations (ICCR) and auditioned artist of Bangalore Doordarshan. They are also members of the International Dance council (CID, UNESCO).

 

Hari and Chethana have performed as a part of the cultural exchange program, such as:

 

Bali Yatra- Indonesia, Thailand, Singapore and Malaysia, “Shakuntalam” a Kathak dance drama, at Washington DC and Canada, “Swapna Sakaar”- performed in the presence of the Foreign Secretary, Ms.Nirupama Rao.
