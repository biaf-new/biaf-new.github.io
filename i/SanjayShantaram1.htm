Sanjay shantaram, as a child prodigy, entered the cultural field at the tender age of 7. Born to the most charming and popular dental surgeon Dr. M. S. Shantaram, Sanjay was inspired by the film “Shankarabharanam”, which meant a stepping stone to learn and pursue bharathanatyam. His mother Mrs.Swarna Shantaram, who initially admitted him to dance classes in the year 1980 under the tutelage of Guru Smt. G.S. Rajalakshmi, was enthralled by the way he showed remarkable progress and saw no way back. He secured the first rank for the state in his junior grade bharathanatyam dance examination conducted by the Karnataka secondary education board. His entry into the field of dance opened the doors to his entry into the field of acting. He thus began as a child actor by starring in his first film directed by Sri Hunsur Krishnamurthy titled “Bhaktha Gnynana Deva” in 1981.

Henceforth offers poured in as he continued to act despite the initial hesitation of his parents.

      He then on continued to act in both Kannada and Telugu films as a very popular child actor having acted with eminent directors like B.S. Ranga, K. Balachander,

Sri Renuka Sharma, Sri K.V. Raju, Sri T. Krishna (Telegu)

Sri .D. Rjendra babu etc, in jubilee films like “ Eradu rekhegalu”, “Maneye Manthralaya”, “Muthaide Bhagya”,

“Thayinudi”, “Benkiyalli Aralidha Huvu” a few to mention.

He then succeeded in securing first rank in his senior grade dance examination conducted by the Karnataka secondary education board in the year 1988 proving to his parents that he could well both culturally and academically when he scored 80% in his tenth grade exams at the same time.

           His achievements in competitions are innumerous. He has won the first place several times in both state and national dance competitions the most popular one being “All India dance competition “ conducted by the navya nataka samithi – Hyderabad in the year 1992 for which he secured the gold medal. He also is a recipient of the dance scholarship awarded by the government of Karnataka and is a regular artist auditioned by the national network – doordarshan. He then continued his training in the field of bharathanatyam under late Guru Smt Narmada and kuchipudi under Guru Smt. Sunanda Devi. His film “Shabari Malai Swamy Ayappa” in the year 1990 directed by Sri .Renuka Sharma broke all the records and his character of ayappa won him immense popularity, which is popular even today.

               His noteworthy achievement was when he was awarded the degree as the Bachelors of dental surgery in the year 1997.

         He opened his dance institution “Shiva Priya School of dance” in the year 1989 which then started of with a handful of students and now teaches more than 150 students in the divine form of bharathanatyam, kuchipudi and folk dances of India. His student P. Raghuraj secured the first rank for the state in the senior grade dance examination making his teacher proud. 

       Sanjay has performed in the U.S.A  from the year 1986 for several organizations like the kannada koota, triveni sangha, telugu association, American association, shakthi performing arts centre etc having achieved critical acclaim and applause by one and all. He has toured Muscat where he performed for an eight day Indian folk dance festival. In 1998 he toured the united kingdom under a contract with The Indian dance –Wales and performed at Cardiff, London. Manchester, Birmingham etc. He has also performed in Singapore and in each place left the audience spellbound and extremely happy.
