About US

  • Celebrating the spirit of Bangalore!'Gateway to the Cultural Extravaganza for 10 Days'

BIAF’s Aim is to celebrate the cultural spirit of Bangalore. BIAF brings together world class performers from the field of Music, Dance, Theatre & Visual Arts.
BIAF –The Annual cultural extravaganza features some of the world renowned artists from across the globe.

AIM - Artistes’ Introspective Movement a federation of artists with progressive attitude towards arts which is headed by internationally renowned Veena Maestro Dr.Suma Sudhindra and renowned Kuchipudi Exponent Veena Murthy Vijay. Since its inception in year 2003 AIM has carried on projects and festivals in the field of Dance, Music, theatre & arts by various world renowned artists. AIM has also produced a 13 part life style tele serial “One State Many Worlds” presenting the heritage sites of Karnataka and performances by legendary Artists. Since last six years Aim has been successfully organizing one of India’s ten biggest Arts Festivals “Bengaluru International Arts Festival” BIAF from 2008.

BIAF celebrates & presents Art and Art projects in a global perspective. This grand endeavor over last 7 years has created a platform and opportunity to the Local artists as well as Artists across India and world over to interact and perform in an international context.

BIAF brings world class performers in Classical Music, Theater, Folk and Classical Dance from various parts of the World in this pioneering endeavor - Sufi Music or Classical Music, Western or Fusion, Creativity is unlimited.

The theme for BIAF is ‘HARMONY’, Culture & art are the best means of building bridges to bring harmony among people.

Standing for innovation & integrity, AIM- BIAF serves as a great magnet for creating a vortex of energy in the space and the community at large. A part of funds collected will be utilized for welfare of the community.

Over these 7 years, BIAF has been recognized as one of the major global cultural events, just as any international festival the world over.

AIM is deeply grateful to all the sponsors and advisory board members for their undivided support and belief in us.
We invite you to partner in this International Cultural scenario through the support of Sponsorship.

We will be glad to provide you with more information. Looking forward to your synergic support in this journey of propagation of arts.

OUR GOAL:

* To create a platform for artists from within the country and from outside to be able to interact, collaborate and perform together.This will help bring out the essence of a co-existence through Harmony.
* To build and regenerate links between society and the artists through public-private partnerships.
* To help needy artists by donating a part of the funds to ‘Arogyadhara’ an artist’s welfare fund and health helpline to the needy artists.
* To create environmental awareness and do our bit in contributing towards our Garden City .
* To attract Artists, Art Lovers, Business groups and Tourists… thus creating a common platform for artists and sponsors to interact , hence opening up new avenues for branding / promotional opportunities.
